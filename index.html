About PM-Uday

DDA PM-Uday Yojana is a scheme launched by the government of India for Delhi citizens under which people with flats and houses in unauthorized colonies can apply online and get ownership rights. PM-Uday registration online portal is already active for the registry of all flats and houses falling under unauthorized colonies. According to the PM Uday news, one needs to follow the PM-Uday registration process and get the registry papers by paying a nominal fee.

As per the reports, around 40 lakhs of people live in unauthorized colonies in Delhi and will get the ownership of their houses and flats under PM Uday awas adhikar yojana. Apart from this, people can also get a loan against their flats and houses. Before applying for PM-Uday Scheme, you should know all the details regarding DDA-PM Uday Scheme, PM Uday online registration, and don’t forget to check the PM Uday unauthorized colony list. If you have any query regarding the PM Uday unauthorized colony registration process or any issue during the PM-Uday login process, contact the PM Uday help desk by making a call on the PM-Uday customer care number.

What is PM-Uday Scheme?

PM Uday Scheme stands for Prime Ministers Unauthorized Colonies in Delhi Awas Adhikar Yojana. It was passed by the Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha of the Parliament to facilitate regulations of the colonies and handing over the ownership rights of the properties to the anxious residents. PM Uday awas adhikar yojana is a step towards ensuring dignity in the lives of thousands of families.

You must be thinking about how you can get the ownership rights of your flat or home if you are living in an unauthorized colony in Delhi. DDA PM-Uday Yojana is specially launched to resolve your problem. The PM-Uday scheme provides ownership rights to the people living in unauthorized colonies of Delhi. A portal has been developed for the registration of homes and filing application form for ownership rights in unauthorized territories. PM-Uday Delhi scheme is being implemented by the Delhi Development Authority (DDA) on a priority basis. Any person who wants to get detailed information about the PM-Uday scheme can directly contact the PM Uday help desk by calling the official PM Uday customer care number. The people can also contact us by calling on our support number, and we will provide them every detail about the PM Uday awas adhikar yojana. Let’s discuss all the details about the PM-Uday registration process and the documents required to apply for the PM-Uday registration online.

What Documents required with Application File?

If you are among those who want to get your house’s ownership rights or flat, you have to do the PM-Uday registration online along with the following documents. The documents required for DDA PM-Uday Yojana are as follows:

  • Possession Document
  • Payment Reciept
  • Latest Ownership Document / Latest GPA
  • Electricity Bill
  • Property Tax Mutation Document
  • Previous Chain of Documents in Serial ordert
  • Documentary Proof of construction before 1st January 2015. (In case of Built-up properties)
  • Any other Ownership document

If you possess these documents, you can quickly complete the PM-Uday registration process.
